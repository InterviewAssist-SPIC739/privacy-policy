# Privacy Policy - InterviewAssist
Last updated: March 2025

Welcome to **InterviewAssist**, a platform designed to help students and alumni share interview experiences and prepare for career opportunities. Your privacy is important to us.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
- **Account Details:** Name (First and Last), Primary Email, Secondary Email.
- **Authentication:** Password (stored securely/encrypted).
- **Profile Information:** Phone number (for 2FA/Profile), Major, Graduation Year, Current Year, Bio, Profile Picture, LinkedIn URL, Current Company, and Designation.

### 1.2 User-Generated Content
- **Interview Experiences:** Details about your interview rounds, difficulty levels, and advice shared with the community.
- **Questions & Answers:** Questions you ask and answers you provide on the platform.
- **Interactions:** "Helpful" votes and saved items.

### 1.3 Device & Usage Data
- **Logs:** App activity logs and error reports.
- **Security:** Data related to Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) if enabled.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- **Service Provision:** To create and manage your account and profile.
- **Community Interaction:** To display your shared experiences and contributions to other users (Students/Alumni).
- **Personalization:** To show relevant interview experiences and company details.
- **Security:** To protect your account via 2FA and secure login processes.
- **Communication:** To send important updates regarding your account or the platform.

## 3. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services
We do not sell your personal data. We may use the following third-party services:
- **Hosting/Backend:** To store and process data securely.
- **Image Hosting:** For profile picture storage.
- **LinkedIn:** If you choose to link your profile.

## 4. Your Rights
- **Access & Update:** You can update your profile information within the app.
- **Account Deletion:** You can request to delete your account through the app settings.
- **Security Control:** You can enable or disable Two-Factor Authentication.

## 5. Security
We implement HTTPS for data transfer and encryption for sensitive data like passwords.

## 6. Children's Privacy
InterviewAssist is intended for university students and professionals. It is not intended for children under 13.

## 7. Updates to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected within the application.

## 8. Contact Us
If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at:
