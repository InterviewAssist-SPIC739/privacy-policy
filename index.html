Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

Welcome to InterviewAssist. Your privacy is a priority for us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Personal Data

  • Account Details: Name, professional email addresses, and encrypted passwords.
  • Profile Details: Phone number, academic major, graduation year, bio, and professional links (e.g., LinkedIn).
  • Professional Identity: Current company, designation, and areas of specialization.

1.2 Content & Interaction

  • Shared Experiences: Interview insights, difficulty ratings, and preparation strategies shared with the community.
  • Questions & Answers: Interactions within the student and alumni forums.
  • Activity: Helpful votes, saved companies, and bookmarks.

2. Use of Information

We use your data to provide a seamless experience, including:

  • Managing your professional profile and account security.
  • Facilitating mentor-student interactions (Alumni to Student).
  • Improving our recommendation algorithms for interview preparation.
  • Ensuring platform security through Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).

3. Secure Processing

We do not sell your personal information. Data is used solely for platform functionality and is protected by industry-standard encryption (HTTPS). We use professional third-party services for secure backend hosting and image management.

4. Your Controls

You have full autonomy over your data:

  • Edit Profile: Update your information anytime in the settings.
  • Security: Enable or disable 2FA at your discretion.
  • Deletion: Request account and data deletion through the app dashboard.
