Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

At InterviewAssist, we believe transparency is the foundation of trust. This detailed Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users—from students discovering opportunities to alumni sharing their professional journey.

1.1 Voluntary Information

  • Identity Data: Full name, university email, and professional role.
  • Profile Metadata: Phone number, academic major, graduation year, and professional biography.
  • Professional Links: URLs to external professional profiles like LinkedIn or GitHub.

1.2 Content & Contributions

  • Knowledge Sharing: Detailed logs of interview experiences, including company names, roles, difficulty levels, and round-by-round descriptions.
  • Community Activity: Questions asked in forums, answers provided, and "helpful" validation votes.

1.3 Automatically Collected Data

  • Technical Logs: IP addresses, browser type, device identifiers, and operating system information.
  • Usage Metrics: Time spent on specific screens, interaction patterns, and crash reports via Firebase.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data enables us to maintain a secure and helpful community for career growth.

  • Service Delivery: To personalize your dashboard and recommend relevant interview content.
  • Platform Security: To verify identities and facilitate secure Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).
  • Communication: To notify you about responses to your questions or updates to companies you follow.
  • Analytics: To perform internal research and improve the platform's user experience.

3. Third-Party Services

We partner with proven service providers to ensure platform stability and security:

  • Google Firebase: Used for secure authentication, real-time database management, and crash reporting.
  • Cloud Hosting: Our backend is hosted on secure, encrypted cloud infrastructure.
  • Analytical Tools: We use anonymized analytics to understand how users interact with our features.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Retention Philosophy

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. If you choose to delete your account, your personal identifying information is removed from our production databases within 30 days.

Note: Shared interview experiences may be anonymized and retained to ensure the community continues to benefit from the shared knowledge.

5. International Data Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Regardless of your location, we respect your right to control your data:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can correct inaccurate or incomplete data via your profile settings.
  • Right to Portability: You can request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can request full account deletion at any time.

6. Security Measures

We implement multi-layered security protocols:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS and at rest in our databases.
  • Access Control: Administrative access is strictly limited and monitored.
  • Regular Audits: We perform periodic security reviews to stay ahead of potential threats.

7. Children's Privacy

InterviewAssist is not designed for or marketed to individuals under the age of 13. If we discover we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.
